Mesa County Partners - Website Redesign

Taking clunky IA and design and streamlining it for identified users.

User Research

The main insight gained was their need for quality over quantity of mentors.

Based on the stakeholder interview I based my design centered around recruiting mentors and made the path for getting further information and submitting interest clear and concise.

The designed was changed based on the interviews to focus on resources for mentors and prospective mentors.

Problem Statement

Mesa County Partners was designed to connect new mentors like John with at-risk youth in order to provide them with a positive role model. We have observed that Mesa County Partners does not clearly display resources available for mentors on their website, which causes new users like John to feel that they may not be supported through the process.
